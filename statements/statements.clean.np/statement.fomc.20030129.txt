      the federal open market committee decided today to keep its target for the federal funds rate unchanged at 1 14 percent   oil price premiums and other aspects of geopolitical risks have reportedly fostered continued restraint on spending and hiring by businesses  however the committee believes that as those risks lift as most analysts expect the accommodative stance of monetary policy coupled with ongoing growth in productivity will provide support to an improving economic climate over time  in these circumstances the committee believes that against the background of its long run goals of price stability and sustainable economic growth and of the information currently available the risks are balanced with respect to the prospects for both goals for the foreseeable future
